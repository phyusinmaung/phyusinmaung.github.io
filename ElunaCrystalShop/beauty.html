    How to Use Gua Sha & Face Roller

    Step 1 : Prep Your Skin
    Apply a facial oil or serum to help the tool glide smoothly

    Step 2 : Start with the Neck
    Using gentle upward strokes, glide the tool from the base of your neck toward your jawline

    Step 3 : Jawline & Chin
    Sweep from the center of your chin outward toward your ears to sculpt and define

    Step 4 : Cheeks
    Glide from the sides of your nose outward across your cheeks toward your temples

    Step 5 : Under Eye
    With very light pressure, sweep outward to reduce puffiness and smooth fine lines

    Step 6 : Forehead
    Glide upward from your eyebrows to your hairline to lift and smooth wrinkles

    Tip: Repeat each stroke 5–10 times per area. Always use gentle pressure
